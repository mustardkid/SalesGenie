# 🚀 Dealership Pilot Plan (St. George)

**Target Dealership**: The UTV Pros / Auto dealer operated by Jordan and Brandon Kee (St. George, UT).
**Duration**: 14 Days
**Objective**: Validate that salespeople will actually use the tool in real-world scenarios, and measure if it impacts their confidence and close rate.

---

## Pre-Pilot (Days -7 to 0)
1. **Roster Setup**: Collect the names, specialties, and baseline close rates of 3 to 5 participating salespeople.
2. **Inventory Sync**: Ensure the tool accurately decodes the specific types of vehicles on their lot (e.g., UTVs vs Trucks).
3. **Kickoff Meeting (30 mins)**: 
   - Demo the app scanning a VIN and generating a pitch.
   - Explain the 4 DISC personality types simply.
   - Give them the URL and have them save it to their phone home screens.

## Week 1: Adoption & Calibration (Days 1 to 7)
**Goal**: Get them to run the app at least once per day.
- **Rule**: Salespeople must run a D-001 (Full Sales Brief) on at least two walk-in leads or phone leads per day.
- **Friction Logging**: Monitor the backend. Are they getting timeouts? Are VINs failing to decode?
- **Mid-Week Check-in (Text)**: *"Hey Brandon, saw you ran 4 pitches yesterday. Were the objection handlers for that Analytical guy accurate?"*
- **Adjustment**: Tweak the `PERSONALITY_RULES` in the prompt if the pitches sound too robotic or don't match the dealership's vibe.

## Week 2: Measurement & ROI (Days 8 to 14)
**Goal**: Measure qualitative and quantitative impact.
- Focus on the newer salespeople (greenpeas). Are they leaning on the tool for vehicle specs?
- Are managers using the Sales Matchmaker feature to distribute leads?
- **End of Week Survey**:
  1. Did the app give you a fact about a vehicle you didn't know?
  2. Did classifying the buyer's personality change how you spoke to them?
  3. Did the app help save a deal from walking?

## Post-Pilot Debrief (Day 15)
- **Review Dashboard**: Show them the data. "You had 45 walk-ins. 60% were 'Drivers'. You've been pitching them like 'Friendlies'."
- **The Ask**: Transition them to a paid Tier 2 subscription, or collect a glowing video testimonial in exchange for a free extended license to use in marketing.
