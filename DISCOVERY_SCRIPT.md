# 📞 Dealership Discovery Script

Use this script when cold-calling, Zoom-pitching, or meeting with Dealership General Managers (GMs) or General Sales Managers (GSMs).

## 1. The Hook (15 seconds)
"Hey [Name], I'm working on a project that gives brand new sales reps the inventory knowledge of a 10-year veteran, and tells them exactly what to say based on the customer's personality. If I could show you how it works on your phone in 60 seconds, would you take a look?"

## 2. Uncovering the Pain (The Discovery Questions)
*Don't pitch yet. Find out where they are bleeding money.*

1. **Ramp Time**: *"When you hire a new rep, how long does it take before you actually trust them to walk the lot without killing a deal because they don't know the vehicle specs?"*
2. **Process**: *"How do you currently decide which salesperson gets the next up? Is it a round-robin, or are you matching specific customers to specific reps?"*
3. **Lost Deals**: *"When a deal walks off the lot, what's usually the reason? Is it price, or did the rep just rub them the wrong way?"*
4. **CRM Adoption**: *"Be honest — how much do your reps hate using your current CRM? Are they actually putting notes in it while they are with the customer?"*

## 3. The Demo (The "Aha" Moment)
"Pull out your phone. Go to this URL. Type in the VIN of that truck sitting out your window."
*(Wait for them to do it).*
"Now, imagine the customer looking at it is an engineer who cares about numbers and towing capacity. Tap 'Analytical'."
*(Wait 5 seconds for the pitch to generate).*
"Read that objection handler at the bottom. How much more confident is your greenest rep going to be if that's in their pocket?"

## 4. Closing the Pilot
"I'm not asking you to rip out your CRM or sign a year contract. I'm looking for 3 progressive dealerships to run a 14-day pilot. Give this to your 3 newest reps. If it doesn't help them save at least one deal next week, we shake hands and walk away. Deal?"
